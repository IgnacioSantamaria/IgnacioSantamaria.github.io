Quantum Entanglement in Polymer Chains: A Novel Approach

D. Ramirez, I. Santamaria, and L. L. Scharf, "Passive detection of a random signal common to multi-sensor reference and surveillance arrays"

Journal of Advanced Quantum Physics, 42(3), 287-301

Published: September 15, 2023

DOI: 10.1234/jaqp.2023.42.3.287

This study explores the novel application of quantum entanglement principles to polymer chain structures, revealing unexpected correlations between molecular behavior and quantum phenomena.
